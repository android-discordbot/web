All Commands

ping

check ping from bot to discord

#ping

rules

send a rule in general

#rules

youtube

send a youtube link but only works on a specific role

#youtube

kick

kick a member from your server
kick : remove a member from your discord server, but that member can join again at any time

#kick @somebody123

ban

ban a member from your server
ban : you need to unban that member so that member can join to your discord server again

#ban @somebody123

clear

delete a message at once
- there is a limit (100 messsages), also you cannot delete a message that is above 14 days -

#clear 20 | #c 20

mute

this command put that member on a Mute role so that he can still view the channel but cannot chat
- if you don't have a 'Mute' role you can create it first -

#mute @somebody123 | #m @somebody123

timed mute

this works the same as the mute command, but you add a timer on how many seconds that member is being muted

#mute @somebody123 4s | #m @somebody123 4s

unmute

this unmute a member by removing it from the 'Mute' role

#unmute @somebody123 4s | #um @somebody123

help

shows all of the available commands

#help | #h

help music

shows the music commands

#help music | #h music

image

sends an image from google using the keyword that you typed

#image car | #i car

reactionrole

sends an embed with a reaction, so that when you click the reaction it will add it on that role
only works on a specific server :(

#reactionrole | #reactionrole2 | #reactionrole3

gif

send a gif from tenor. You can add a keyword too

#gif car

giphy

just like the gif command but instead this is from giphy

#giphy car | #gip car

hey

say hey command

#hey

avatar

see the bigger profile picture of a member. You can tag a member to get that specific avatar

#avatar | #pp @somebody123

weather

sends a weather forecast
- you can change 'jakarta' to the city that you want -

#weather jakarta

meme

send a meme from a subreddit

#meme

ticket

ticket system
- create a private channel to talk with the admin, later you can delete the channel when you're done -

#ticket @somebody123

invite

sends an invite link so that the bot can join to your server

#invite

status

change the bot status
- it can be 'playing' 'streaming' 'watching' or 'listening' you can add anything after that -

#stats playing game | #stats watching netflix | #stats listening commands

guess the number

play guess the number game
- you must guess a number between 1 - 100 for 1 minute that's it -

#guessthenumber | #gtn

emojify

turns any text that you typed into an emoji

#emojify hello





























Music Commands

play

play a music from youtube

#play lofi | #p lofi

playskip

play a music and then add it to the top of the queue and skip to it

#playskip lofi | #ps lofi

stop

stop playing music and leave the voice channel

#stop | #st | #dc

queue

see the list of music that is on the queue

#queue | #q

skip

skip playing music

#skip | #sk | #s

shuffle

shuffle the queue

#shuffle | #sf

loop

loop playing music
loop 1 for repeating the song
loop 2 for repeating the queue
loop 0 for disabled

#loop 1 | #repeat 1

pause

pause playing music

#pause

resume

resume paused music
(sometimes broken)

#resume | #continue

seek

fast forward to specific part of the music

#seek 10 | #jump 10

autoplay

autoplay related music when there is no queue. Defaults to OFF

#autoplay | #ap

nowplaying

see what song is playing and song current time

#nowplaying | #np
